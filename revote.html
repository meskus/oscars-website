Nominees and Winners of the 2020 Big Five Oscars

  • Best Picture
    1. Parasite (Gisaengchung), (Winner)
      • Producers: Kwak Sin-ae & Bong Joon-ho
    2. Ford v Ferrari
      • Producers: Peter Chernin, Jenno Topping, James Mangold
    3. The Irishman
      • Producers: Martin Scorsese, Robert De Niro, Jane Rosenthal, Emma Tillinger Koskoff
    4. Jojo Rabbit
      • Producers: Carthew Neal, Taika Waititi, Chelsea Winstanley
    5. Joker
      • Producers: Todd Phillips, Bradley Cooper, Emma Tillinger Koskoff
    6. Little Women
      • Producers: Amy Pascal
    7. Marriage Story
      • Producers: Noah Baumbach & David Heyman
    8. 1917
      • Producers: Sam Mendes, Pippa Harris, Jayne-Ann Tenggren, Callum McDougall
    9. Once Upon a Time in Hollywood
      • Producers: David Heyman, Shannon McIntosh, Quentin Tarantino
  • Best Director
    1. Bong Joon-ho, Parasite (Winner)
    2. Sam Mendes, 1917
    3. Todd Phillips, Joker
    4. Martin Scorsese, The Irishman
    5. Quentin Tarantino, Once Upon a Time in Hollywood
  • Best Actress
    1. Renée Zellweger, Judy (Winner)
    2. Cynthia Erivo, Harriet
    3. Scarlett Johannson, Marriage Story
    4. Saoirse Ronan, Little Women
    5. Charlize Theron, Bombshell
  • Best Actor
    1. Joaquin Phoenix, Joker (Winner)
    2. Antonio Banderas, Pain and Glory
    3. Leonardo DiCaprio, Once Upon a Time in Hollywood
    4. Adam Driver, Marriage Story
    5. Jonathan Pryce, The Two Popes
  • Best Original Screenplay
    1. Bong Joon-ho & Han Jin-won, Parasite (Winner)
    2. Rian Johnson, Knives Out
    3. Noah Baumbach, Marriage Story
    4. Sam Mendes & Krysty Wilson-Cairns, 1917
    5. Quentin Tarantino, Once Upon a Time in Hollywood
  • Best Adapted Screenplay
    1. Taika Waititi, Jojo Rabbit (Winner)
    2. Steven Zaillian, The Irishman
    3. Todd Phillips & Scott Silver, Joker
    4. Greta Gerwig, Little Women
    5. Anthony McCarten, The Two Popes
