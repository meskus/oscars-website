2021 Academy Awards

The Awards Show

Due to COVID-related concerns, the awards show was postponed from February 28th to April 25th. There has been no confirmation whether the show will be an in-person or remote event. The Dolby Theatre has been used for the Academy Awards show since 2002.

Nominations (So Far)

The Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences has announced shortlists in nine categories for the 93rd Academy Awards:

  1. Documentary Feature
  2. Documentary Short Subject
  3. International Feature Film
  4. Makeup and Hairstyling
  5. Music (Original Score)
  6. Music (Original Song)
  7. Animated Short Film
  8. Live Action Short Film
  9. Visual Effects
2021 Shortlist Announcement

Possible Big Five Award Nominees

  • Chadwick Boseman of “Black Panther” fame may be nominated for Best Actor posthumously (for his role in “Ma Rainey’s Black Bottom.”)
  • “Mank” has the most Golden Globes nominations (6) this year.
  • Three female directors (two of them women of color) were nominated for Golden Globes Best Director, the first time more than one woman was nominated for the award. Chloé Zhao for “Nomadland,” Regina King for “One Night in Miami,” and Emerald Fennell for “Promising Young Woman.”
  • Riz Ahmed could be the first Muslim to be nominated for Best Actor (for his role in “Sound of Metal.”)
