Glossary

Big Five
The “Big Five” Academy Awards are the Oscars for Best Picture, Best Director, Best Actor, Best Actress, and Best Screenplay (either Best Original Screenplay or Best Adapted Screenplay). —https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_Big_Five_Academy_Award_winners_and_nominees
Snub
An expected recognition (in the form of a nomination or award) that is not given by the Academy. —https://www.themarysue.com/what-is-a-snub/
